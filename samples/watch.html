The Art of Making

Crafted with Obsessive Precision

Every Chronos timepiece is the result of over 600 hours of meticulous handcrafting by our master watchmakers in Geneva. Each component is finished to tolerances measured in microns.

We use only the finest materials — sapphire crystal, 18k gold, and movements assembled under 10x magnification — because we believe that what you cannot see matters just as much as what you can.
